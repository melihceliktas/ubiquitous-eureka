Items

In Knight Online World, there are many types of items. These are; swords, clubs, spears, shields, bows, crossbows, maces, staffs, daggers, jamadars, belts, earings, necklaces and rings.

Swords, clubs and spears are for warriors and portus-kurians.

Bows, crossbows, daggers and jamadars for rogues.

Maces and shields for priests.

Staffs are for Mages.

And the accessories are belts, earings, necklaces and rings.

Also all classes have classifed armors.

Iron Necklace is one of the rarest items in the game.
